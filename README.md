# Problem Statement

How might we use Artificial Intelligence to estimate and analyze students' carbon footprints so that students can make environmentally sustainable decisions and contribute to climate action?

Students generate carbon emissions through transportation, electricity usage, food consumption, water usage, and purchasing habits. Most students are unaware of the environmental impact of these activities.

Without proper awareness:

• Energy consumption increases

• Unsustainable transportation choices continue

• Carbon emissions remain high

• Environmental sustainability goals become difficult to achieve

An AI-powered solution is needed to analyze student lifestyle data and provide sustainability insights.

# AI Solution Overview

The proposed Carbon Footprint Tracker uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to estimate carbon emissions generated by students.

The system analyzes:

• Transportation Methods

• Food Habits

• Housing Type

• Electricity Consumption

• Water Usage

• Spending Behavior

• Recycling Habits

The AI model predicts the student's carbon footprint and identifies factors contributing to higher emissions.

Students receive sustainability insights and recommendations for reducing their environmental impact.
