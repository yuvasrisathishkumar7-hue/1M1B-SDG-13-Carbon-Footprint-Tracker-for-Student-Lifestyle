<b><h1 align="center">CARBON FOOTPRINT TRACKER FOR STUDENT LIFESTYLE USING AI (KAGGLE DATASET)</h1>

<hr>

<h2>PROJECT OVERVIEW</h2>

<p>
The <b>Carbon Footprint Tracker for Student Lifestyle Using AI</b> is an AI-powered sustainability analytics project that estimates and analyzes the carbon emissions generated by students through their daily lifestyle activities.
</p>

<p>
The system evaluates important sustainability factors such as <b>transportation habits, food consumption patterns, electricity usage, water consumption, housing conditions, spending behavior, and recycling practices</b>.
</p>

<p>
Using <b>Machine Learning algorithms</b>, the project predicts student carbon emissions and provides personalized recommendations to encourage environmentally responsible behavior and sustainable living practices.
</p>

<p>
An <b>interactive dashboard</b> is developed using <b>Plotly</b> to visualize sustainability metrics, carbon emission trends, and lifestyle impacts through dynamic charts and graphs.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>PROBLEM STATEMENT</h2>

<p>
Students contribute significantly to carbon emissions through daily activities such as transportation, electricity consumption, food habits, water usage, and purchasing behavior.
</p>

<p>
However, many students are unaware of their environmental impact and lack tools that can help them understand and reduce their carbon footprint.
</p>

<p>
Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent AI-powered system that can analyze lifestyle data, estimate carbon emissions, and provide actionable sustainability recommendations to promote climate-conscious decision-making.
</p>
