PROJECT TITLE	CARBON FOOTPRINT TRACKER FOR STUDENT LIFESTYLE USING AI
PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Carbon Footprint Tracker for Student Lifestyle Using AI is an intelligent sustainability solution designed to estimate, analyze, and reduce carbon emissions generated by students through their daily lifestyle activities.

The system leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) algorithms to evaluate lifestyle factors such as transportation habits, food consumption patterns, electricity usage, water consumption, housing conditions, spending behavior, and recycling practices.

Based on these factors, the AI model predicts the student's carbon footprint and provides personalized sustainability recommendations to encourage environmentally responsible behavior.

This project supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 13 – Climate Action, by promoting awareness and sustainable decision-making among students.
